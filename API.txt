
### 📬 **Contact APIs**

1. **Add Contact**  
   `POST http://localhost:5000/api/contact`  
   ➤ Submit a new contact form entry.

2. **Get All Contacts**  
   `GET http://localhost:5000/api/contact`  
   ➤ Retrieve all contact form entries.

3. **Delete Contact by Email**  
   `DELETE http://localhost:5000/api/contact/email/:email`  
   ➤ Delete a specific contact entry by providing the email.

---

### 💼 **Experience APIs**

1. **Add Experience**  
   `POST http://localhost:5000/api/experience`  
   ➤ Add a new work experience.

2. **Get All Experiences**  
   `GET http://localhost:5000/api/experience`  
   ➤ Retrieve all work experiences.

3. **Update Experience by ID**  
   `PUT http://localhost:5000/api/experience/:id` 
/experience/company/CompanyName 
   ➤ Update an experience entry by its ID.

4. **Delete Experience by ID**  
   `DELETE http://localhost:5000/api/experience/:id`  
/experience/company/CompanyName
   ➤ Remove a specific experience entry by ID.

---

### 🚀 **Project APIs**

1. **Add Project**

2. **Get All Projects**  
   `GET http://localhost:5000/api/project`

3. **Update Project by ID**  
   `PUT http://localhost:5000/api/project/:id`  
   ➤ Update a specific project using its ID.

4. **Delete Project by ID**  
   `DELETE http://localhost:5000/api/project/:id`  
   ➤ Delete a specific project entry by ID.
